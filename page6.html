 Tim Berners-Lee invented  the Web with HTML as its publishing language in 1993. The World Wide Web began life in the place where you would least expect it: at CERN, research institute of technology  in  Switzerland.
Who is the biggest hacker in the world?
Kevin David Mitnick (born August 6, 1963) is an American computer security consultant, author, and convicted hacker. He is best known for his high-profile 1995 arrest and five years in prison for various computer and communications-related crimes.
